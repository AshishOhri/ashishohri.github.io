Project Gallery

Workinout

Workinout is an AI fitness trainer app equipped with pose correction with the purpose to make home workouts injuryfree, accessible and inclusive for all. It estimates your exercise pose using your camera and gives voice commands to correct your posture.

Snap N Reuse

The application was created for the purpose of waste reduction. The app takes images of waste items as input, passes them to an ML model for predictions, and then gives recommendations through curated instructables on how to reuse that item to make something useful.

Self Balancing Bot

The project is a two wheeled self balancing bot that has joystick control and is also able to deliver objects from one place to another.

Stock Alert App

The stock alert app creates alerts on your screen whenever stocks reflect your technical analysis conditionality. The app was created using selenium and tkinter.

Bolrace

A full stack horse racing website. The webapp was created for a client running a horse race business and provides the entire information regarding the previous and upcoming races in a clean and compact way.

Custom Face Unlock System

A face unlock system was created through the use of openCV to store faces and at the same time differentiate between a live face and a photograph by incorporating eye blink detection system.
